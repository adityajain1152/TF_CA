About Us


Techfest, IIT Bombay was initiated in 1998 with a mission to promote technology, scientific thinking, and innovation. Today, Techfest is Asia's Largest Science and Technology Festival with an annual footfall of 1.75 lakhs and a reach of over 2500 colleges including 500+ inter universities. With a plethora of workshops, competitions, lectures, conferences, exhibitions, performances and various other events, Techfest is the place to be for all tech lovers: experts and newbies alike.
